About TrendCart

A multi-service platform built for shopping, food ordering, and movie booking.

TrendCart brings together three everyday flows into one streamlined experience. The platform is designed to help users discover products, place food orders from multiple hotels, and book movie tickets with a cleaner interface and faster movement between browsing and checkout.

Products

The shopping area is designed for category browsing, cleaner visual presentation, and a more polished path from discovery to cart and payment.

Foods

The food section groups menus by hotel so users can explore a wider set of dishes while keeping ordering simple and fast.

Movies

The movie flow focuses on theatre selection, seat booking, and ticket confirmation so cinema booking feels like a complete part of the platform rather than a separate tool.
